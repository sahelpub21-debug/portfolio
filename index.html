GLOVO Morocco

Navigated complex diplomatic tensions through strategic crisis communications and the creation of "Humans of Glovo" campaign, transforming potential brand crisis into strengthened local market position.

Client GLOVO
Context Crisis Management
Role Crisis Communications Consultant
Impact 1.3M Organic Reach
[GLOVO Campaign Visual]

Crisis Context

Amid diplomatic tensions between Morocco and Spain following the Polisario leader incident, Spanish brands faced potential boycotts in Morocco. GLOVO, as a Spanish-origin company, needed immediate strategic intervention to protect its market position.

The challenge was to demonstrate GLOVO's deep commitment to Morocco while navigating sensitive political dynamics and maintaining operational continuity.

Strategic Response

01

Localization Strategy

Positioned GLOVO as a brand with deep local ties, emphasizing Moroccan team leadership and commitment to local communities.

02

Humans of Glovo Campaign

Created authentic video series featuring delivery partners sharing their stories, humanizing the brand and building emotional connections.

03

CSR Initiatives

Launched targeted programs focusing on road safety, ethical delivery practices, and improved HR policies for partners.

04

Community Engagement

Amplified local voices and stories, showcasing GLOVO's role in supporting Moroccan entrepreneurs and families.
